EllCrafts

I am Happy to introduce ourselves as M/s Ellcrafts and we are a four year old company, we are an Embedded Design House located in Poonamllee, Chennai. So far we have developed a range of products to our customers who are in Power, Mining, Railways, Cold forging, weighing, and automobile sectors. We can support you with customised and tailor made solutions in Electrical and Electronic Control Systems, also support your legacy systems which may need a service support or indigenisation of a system or Electronic PCBs. We are also an authorised dealer for Kalkitech (Protocol Converters, AMRs), Sumitron (Soldering Station, Magnifiers), Falcon Instruments (All measurement instruments), and SVA Rikkion (Lubrication System). Our dealing range includes Magnetic Induction Lamp which is considered to be the next trend-setter in the Industrial Lighting and considered to be a robust energy saver. Our Esteemed Customers are Neyveli Lignite Corporation, Ramsays Corporation, Merit SCADA Automation, Calsonic Kansei Motherson.
